    Welcome to GlassyAI
    
        
    The ultimate foolproof, AI-powered productivity dashboard.
    
        
      
    

      
    
        
    1. Settings & Customization
    
        
    Click the Gear Icon in the bottom left to change Themes, upload Custom Video Backgrounds, toggle Live Weather animations, and select your Clock style.
    
        
      
    

      
    
        
    2. Native AI Chat
    
        
    GlassyAI has a built-in AI engine with memory context! Paste your API keys in the Settings menu, select your AI from the search bar dropdown, and type. It works perfectly without ever leaving the tab.
    
        
      
    

      
    
        
    3. Tools & Productivity
    
        
    Use the left sidebar for Quick Notes, Tasks, and Dev Tools. Use the bottom right widgets for a Pomodoro Timer and Ambient Zen Sounds.
